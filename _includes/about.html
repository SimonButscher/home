Affiliation & Field of Activity

Since 2018 I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Human-computer Interaction Group of the University of Konstanz. My core research interests are human-computer interaction, usability research, reality-based interfaces, and information visualization. Beyond research, my field of activity ranges from the project lead of third-party projects to teaching activities and the supervision of student research projects. In addition, I have been working as a Freelancer in the field of Software/Database/Data Warehouse Engineering for over 12 years now.

CV

Between 2012 and 2018 I worked as a Research Associate in the Human-computer Interaction Group of the University of Konstanz. Before that, I studied Information Engineering/Computer Science at the University of Konstanz and received my M.Sc. in 2012. During my master studies, I worked as an intern in the field of UX Design at Siemens AG, Corporate Unit Technology. Between 2008 and 2012 I studied Economic Computer Science at the Baden-Wuerttemberg Cooperative State University in Ravensburg and was awarded a Diploma/B.Sc degree. During these studies, I worked at the Systema Deutschland GmbH as a Software Engineer.
